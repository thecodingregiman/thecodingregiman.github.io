About me

This is a site I wanted to start to answer the question of "Why I ride?". My goal is to create a website not only to answer that question and post it to a website. I wanted my journey of cycling to be shared in a aesthetically pleasing way. My goal is to use all the modern day website development design concepts to bring together content in a creative way. All surrounding cycling and a new endeavor I am refering to as cycling photography. Feel free to explore and visit my page where I elaborate on this new concept.

I have only been cycling for a few years. But I definitely enjoy getting out there and riding. Riding gives you the flexiblity to perform an intense workout or ride casually. It allows you to be creative as you want in your workouts.

What is Let's Ride

The purpose is very simple. A central resource for creative photography surrounding cycling. The site uses modern frame works to present content. It can be of events, group rides, commutes, etc. We encourge unique and insanely creative photos of bicycles of all kinds.
